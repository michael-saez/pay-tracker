Premiums logged

A premium earned in a pay period is paid on the base + overage check that starts the next period. LP1 pays 150%, LP2 pays 250% — the Paid column and check totals use the multiplied value.

Pay hours by pay period

Base pay is a contractual 75:00 per pay period = 37:30 per check; anything above 75:00 is overage, settling on the next period's base+overage check. A picked-up JA (junior assignment) adds its nominal credit hours to the Pay total but is paid at 150% — the JA column shows those nominal hours, and the Check Lookup carves them out of overage and pays them at 1.5×.
